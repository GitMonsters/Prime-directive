PROBLEM IDENTIFIED & SOLVED
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Root Cause: Level 2 (multi-agent) constrained at 61.4% despite Phase 1-3 fixes

Investigation revealed THREE bottleneck issues:

1. BROKEN ENERGY ACCURACY COMPONENT
   Problem:   Energy error averaging 0.022 (essentially random noise)
   Impact:    0.3 weight on unreliable signal was dragging down empathy
   Solution:  Removed from formula entirely

2. IDENTICAL COUPLING MATRICES
   Problem:   All agents had deterministic couplings from (i+j) % 3 pattern
   Impact:    coupling_sim always 1.0 (never differentiated agents)
   Solution:  Made couplings seed-dependent with random variation factor

3. WRONG WEIGHTING FORMULA
   Problem:   Over-weighted unreliable energy component
   Impact:    Formula collapsed to ~0.587 even with good state_overlap
   Solution:  New formula: 0.45*overlap + 0.45*coupling_sim + 0.10*mag_sim
